Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | Former President of India

Biography

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and leaders. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a major role in developing India's missile and nuclear programs.

Major Achievements

  • Played key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
  • Led India's missile development programs.
  • Awarded the Bharat Ratna in 1997.
  • Inspired millions of students across India.

Famous Quote

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."
